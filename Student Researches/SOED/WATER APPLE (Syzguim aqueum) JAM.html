WATER APPLE (Syzguim aqueum) JAM 


WATER APPLE (Syzguim aqueum) JAM

By:
Ejoyce P. Barquillo
Apple Jane M. Bordon
Rodelyn J. Moralista
Geneve L. Portosa

March 2015


ABSTRACT

This Study aims to find out the acceptability of sweeteners in preparing water apple jam in terms of appearance, flavor and texture in different proportions. The study was conducted at Pototan National Comprehensive High School, Pototan, Iloilo on February 20, 2015. The study employed experimental method of research. The study has three treatments and for each treatment replications were made; teachers,students and consumers. The sensory evaluation sheet based on a five point Hedonic scale was used. Mean ANOVA was employed in the analysis of the obtained data. The .05 alpha level was used inferential statistics. Study result revealed that in terms of appearance, flavor and texture, Water Apple Jam was highly acceptable. There is no significant of difference in the acceptability of sweeteners in preparing Water Apple Jam in terms of appearance, flavor and texture in different proportions.
